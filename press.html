About Before Leaving

Before Leaving is your complete life organizer — a smart reminder app that helps you never forget anything when leaving home, work, gym, or any location. With weather-aware suggestions, family group sharing, gamification, and support for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac, it transforms staying organized into an enjoyable experience.

Beyond reminders, Before Leaving now includes Family Groups, Pet Care, Plant Tracker, Travel & Trips, Health & Fitness tracking, Bills & Subscriptions management, shared Shopping Lists, and the Home Hub dashboard — making it the ultimate all-in-one companion for your daily life.
