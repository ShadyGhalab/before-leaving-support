How do I create my first reminder?

Creating a reminder is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and tap the + button
  2. Select a location (Home, Work, Gym, etc.) or create a custom one
  3. Add items you want to remember
  4. Set when you want to be reminded (time, day, or recurring)
  5. Tap Save and you're done!
How do I add custom locations?

To add a custom location:

  1. Go to the Locations tab
  2. Tap the + button
  3. Enter a name for your location (e.g., "Parent's House")
  4. Optionally, select an icon to represent it
  5. Tap Save

You can now create reminders for this location!

What's the difference between one-time and recurring reminders?

One-Time Reminders: These trigger once at the specific date and time you set. Perfect for appointments, meetings, or special occasions.

Recurring Reminders: These repeat on a schedule you define‚Äîdaily, weekly, or custom days. Ideal for regular routines like "Every Monday before Work" or "Every day before the Gym."

How do I enable notifications?

When you first install Before Leaving, we'll ask for notification permissions. If you denied them or need to re-enable:

  1. Open your iPhone's Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Before Leaving
  3. Tap Notifications
  4. Toggle Allow Notifications on
  5. Enable Sounds, Badges, and Banners as desired

üîß Troubleshooting

I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

If notifications aren't working, try these steps:

  1. Check that notifications are enabled in iPhone Settings ‚Üí Before Leaving ‚Üí Notifications
  2. Make sure Do Not Disturb and Focus modes aren't blocking notifications
  3. Verify the reminder is set to the correct time/date
  4. Ensure the app isn't force-closed (swipe up and away from the app switcher)
  5. Restart your iPhone
  6. If using Low Power Mode, some notifications may be delayed
My data isn't syncing with my Apple Watch. How do I fix it?

To troubleshoot watch syncing:

  1. Make sure your iPhone and Apple Watch are connected (check Control Center)
  2. Ensure both devices have sufficient battery
  3. Open the Before Leaving app on both devices to trigger a sync
  4. Restart both your iPhone and Apple Watch
  5. Re-install the watch app if issues persist
The app is crashing or running slowly. What can I do?

Try these solutions:

  1. Make sure you're running the latest version of the app (check the App Store for updates)
  2. Restart the app by force-closing and reopening
  3. Restart your iPhone
  4. Free up storage space if your device is nearly full
  5. If the issue persists, contact us with details about when the crash occurs
How do I restore my data after reinstalling the app?

Your data is stored locally by default. If you uninstall the app, local data is deleted. To protect your data:

  • Use iCloud backup to backup your entire device
  • Use the group feature which stores data in the cloud
  • Before uninstalling, export important data if available
Weather suggestions aren't working. Why?

Weather features require location access:

  1. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Location Services
  2. Find Before Leaving and select "While Using the App" or "Always"
  3. Ensure you have an internet connection for weather data
  4. Check that the weather feature is enabled in app Settings
