Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I create a new reminder?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, select a location, add your items, and set when you want to be reminded. It's that simple!

Can I share reminders with my family?

Yes! Create a group, invite family members using an invite code, and share reminders. Everyone stays in sync with real-time updates.

Does the app work with Apple Watch?

Absolutely! Before Leaving has a full companion watchOS app with complications. Check your reminders and mark items complete right from your wrist.

How does weather awareness work?

The app checks the weather forecast for your location and automatically suggests relevant itemsâ€”like an umbrella when rain is expected or sunscreen when UV is high.
